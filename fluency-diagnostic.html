What your coach should listen for in this passage
  • "very," "volume," "we," "walk," "want" — v/w sound clarity
  • "think," "through," "thank," "although," "together" — th sound (dental fricative) placement
  • "patience," "questions," "significant" — consonant cluster and stress handling
  • "quarter's," "clients," "details" — final consonant clarity, not dropped
  • Overall sentence stress — English is stress-timed (some words hit harder than others), not every syllable equal weight
